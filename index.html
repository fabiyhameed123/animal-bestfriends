Animal House Shelter is a non-profit, no-kill
501(c)3 shelter for all breeds of dogs and cats.
We rescue, care for, and find homes for
homeless pets who arrive at AHS for various
reasons, including:

  • Abuse, neglect, or abandonment
  • Owners whose time, income, or situation changes
  • Animals scheduled for euthanasia at other clinics

Looking to bring a pet home?

Our goal is to find loving, stable homes for the pets in our care. Learn more about how you can adopt or foster your new best friend.
